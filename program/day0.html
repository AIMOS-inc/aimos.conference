Day 0 - Monday 20 November

repliCATS workshop

The repliCATS project will run an in-person preconference workshop before AIMOS2023 on Monday 20 November. The goal of repliCATS (Collaborative Assessments for Trustworthy Science) is to build capacity in peer review. This workshop will focus on evaluating the credibility of research in psychology and adjacent disciplines using our structured group deliberation protocol. We will offer assessment grants for all participants funded by a grant from the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation.
