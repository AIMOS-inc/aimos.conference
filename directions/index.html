Directions

The conference will be in the Owen J Wordsworth Room which is on Level 12 of S-Block at the Gardens Point Campus at Queensland University of Technology (QUT), Brisbane.

Photos of S-Block to come.

Places to stay

There are lots of hotels in Southbank, which is just a short walk across the Goodwill Bridge (pedestrian bridge). There are also lots of hotels and apartments in the city, and this will be a short walk down George Street or through the botanical gardens.

Public transport

Public transport information is available on the translink website. Use "QUT Gardens Point Campus" as the "end location".

  • Ferry: The QUT Ferry Terminal is a short walk from S-Block.
  • Buses:
  • Train: Either get off at South Bank and walk across the Goodwill Bridge, or get off at Central and walk through the city.

Weather

According to the Bureau of Meteorology the average November temperatures in Brisbane range from 18 to 28 (degrees C). It can be a wet month, with tropical storms. The bureau forecast is here.
